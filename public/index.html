How can I install a solar plant on my roof ?

Usually it takes 3-4 months from your decission to the first Watt of solar energy in your house.

There are 4 main steps which you need to make on the way to it

1. PROJECT

First of all you need to design a project of a future solar plant. It involves calculation of required capacity and drawing it on paper and in 3D.

2. COORDINATION

Every project must be approved by croatian instances and HEP. You need to send it and also a filled application to these instances.

3. BUYING SOLAR PANELS

After the project is approved you can buy chosen solar panels, inverters and constructions.

4. MOUNTING

Finally you need to mount solar plant on your roof and connect it to electrical network.

Our company does everything from this list turnkey and offers one of the best conditions on the market!
